Biography

I am a Senior Researcher at the Dalle Molle Institute for Artificial Intelligence (IDSIA USI-SUPSI) in Lugano, Switzerland, where I lead the AI and Education pillar within the Responsible AI and Society scientific area and coordinate bioinformatics research activities within the Machine Learning and Artificial Neural Networks research area.

Since joining IDSIA in 2013, I have been involved in both methodological and applied research in artificial intelligence. My research spans trustworthy AI, biomedical research, bioinformatics, machine learning, probabilistic modelling, and interpretable AI systems.

I am a member of the Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics (SIB) within the Machine Learning for Bioinformatics and Personalised Medicine community and a member of COST Action PREMEDICARE. I also serve as Work Package Leader in the Horizon Europe project focused on genome-centric multimodal data integration for personalised cardiovascular medicine.

I co-lead projects on AI literacy in compulsory education, focusing on assessment frameworks, learning pathways, and teacher preparation for responsible AI integration in schools.

I teach probability and statistics, machine learning for bioinformatics, and AI-related topics.
